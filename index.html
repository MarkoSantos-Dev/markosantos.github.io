Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Mark Nelson Santos, a seasoned professional with a passion for Full Stack Web Development, Instructor, and Senior Sales Executive. With a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology majoring in Networking, I've embarked on a journey that's allowed me to seamlessly blend technical expertise with business acumen.

Transitioning into the realm of Instructor, I've had the opportunity to share my knowledge with aspiring developers, guiding them through comprehensive programs and facilitating their entry into the tech industry. Creating a supportive learning environment, I've mentored individuals to grasp web development fundamentals, fostered peer-to-peer learning, and developed e-learning materials to ensure holistic understanding and growth.

My journey also led me to excel in Senior Sales Executive roles, where I honed my skills in customer management, strategic planning, and effective negotiation. Through nurturing productive business relationships and conducting thorough market research, I've consistently driven revenue growth and fostered customer loyalty.

With a robust skill set encompassing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, Laravel, MySQL, and more, I'm equipped to tackle diverse challenges and deliver impactful solutions. Whether it's optimizing server configurations, developing strategic sales plans, or crafting scalable web applications, I thrive on pushing boundaries and exceeding expectations.

Beyond professional endeavors, I'm deeply committed to continuous learning and growth, always seeking new opportunities to expand my horizons and contribute meaningfully to projects and teams.
